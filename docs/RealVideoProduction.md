# Real Video Production Guide

This guide explains how to use the new YouTube category workflow for actual publishable Shorts.

## Recommended menu flow

Inside the YouTube workspace, use:

1. `Create Videos`
2. Choose a category:
   - `Classic Short`
   - `Short with Cached Images`
   - `Story / Entertainment Short`
   - `Market Commentary / Finance Short`
   - `Biblical / Devotional Short`
3. Review the generated project in `outputs/youtube/<project>/`
4. Watch `video.mp4` locally
5. Upload only after final review

## Category guidance

### Classic Short
Use for fast general-purpose content when speed matters more than deep narrative continuity.

Best practices:
- one strong topic
- very short sentences
- simple image flow
- broad audience appeal

### Story / Entertainment Short
Use when retention matters most.

Best practices:
- establish a protagonist quickly
- create one conflict or surprise
- escalate cleanly
- end with payoff or twist
- keep scene continuity strong

### Market Commentary / Finance Short
Use for market news, company updates, business commentary, and economic explainers.

Best practices:
- anchor the narration to real dates, events, prices, or reports
- save real references in `references.txt`
- avoid exaggerated earnings promises
- review wording before upload
- include context if the information is time-sensitive

### Biblical / Devotional Short
Use for faith-based encouragement, reflection, scripture-centered messaging, and devotional storytelling.

Best practices:
- base the message on a real biblical theme or passage
- avoid invented quotes presented as scripture
- keep the tone reverent and calm
- prefer encouragement, reflection, and clarity over sensationalism
- review the final script before upload

## Real-world review checklist

Before publishing a real video:

- confirm `video.mp4` exists in the project folder
- open `script.txt` and verify tone and wording
- open `references.txt` for factual topics
- check `scenes.json` if the topic is sensitive
- confirm `images/` and `scene_videos/` match the message
- watch the exported video from start to finish
- upload only after review

## Asset preservation

Generated outputs are intended to remain stored locally.

Important locations:
- `outputs/youtube/` for final project folders
- `.mp/` for working files and temporary session artifacts
- `outputs/image_library/` for recovered image batches

Generated media should not be treated as disposable if it may be reused later.
